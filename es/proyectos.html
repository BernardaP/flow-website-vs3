Field work, hydrology and social conflict resolution are just part of the story.

Our work on natural resources projects has taken us to many interesting places. We have had the pleasure to meet some of the nicest people who inhabit this planet and to partner with some wonderful companies that value the people as much as the resources removed from the ground. We thank all we have crossed paths with, and we thank you for the opportunity to work together.

Our projects span many sizes, scopes and complexities with industries ranging from mining, oil, hydropower, geothermal and environmental conservation, etc. They are the result of a team effort and the important commitment to water and people.
