Manifesto.

Working together as a team with people, communities and organizations, where water is at the center, to achieve a common goal.

At Flow, we understand every project begins with a desired outcome. We are acutely aware the path to reach that outcome is not always a straight line; it can take many unforeseen twists and turns. Our goal is to work with all involved to ensure the water resources and those who inhabit the land are not compromised, while meeting the unforeseen challenges that come with every project.

Flow is a consultant firm based in Ecuador and working throughout South America. We do our best work at the intersection of hydrology and social conflicts that impact communities where water is central.
